Decentralized and open-source AI are crucial for ensuring both safety and efficiency in artificial intelligence. Decentralization minimizes risks associated with central control, promoting transparency and reducing the possibility of manipulation. Open-source frameworks enable collaborative development, fostering innovation and accountability. This approach enhances the adaptability and robustness of AI models by drawing on diverse perspectives, making the ecosystem more resilient against biases and security vulnerabilities present in centralized systems.
